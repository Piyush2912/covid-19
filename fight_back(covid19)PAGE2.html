What are the symptoms of COVID-19? 


Symptoms can include fever, cough and shortness of breath. In more severe cases, infection can cause pneumonia or breathing difficulties


To prevent the spread of COVID-19:

  1. Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.
  2. Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.
  3. Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.
  4. Stay home if you feel unwell.
  5. Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.

How to Manage Anxiety and Isolation During Quarantine?

  1. Doing one productive thing per day can lead to a more positive attitude.
  2. Set your sights on long-avoided tasks, reorganize, or create something you’ve always wanted to.
  3. This is your chance to slow down and focus on yourself.
  4. Avoid obsessing over endless Coronavirus coverage
  5. Start a new quarantine ritual
